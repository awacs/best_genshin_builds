Zibai pre-release meta analysis

Bro she is probably the easiest T0 character to play in the game lmao

Her DPS is higher than Flins and Neffer granted you have C6 Iiluga and Columbina.

Mavuika is still the DPS Queen but only if you can do the combo properly. Zibai tends to be better than non-combo Mavuika. 

Zibai needs C6 Iiluga to be better than other nod-krai DPS, so good luck with that. 

Columbina is equally important for her damage, although getting C6 Iiluga and Zibai cons tend to be more important than Columbina as C1 Zibai + C6(Aino, Iiluga, Gorou) can make up for the missing Columbina.

TLDR : She is very good meta wise, still doing less damage than Combo Mavuika but more than non-combo Mavuika granted you have her best teams. It's very important to make sure you pull for Bina and C6 Iiluga for her from a meta standpoint
